My Recipe for Cornbread (Proja)

Ingredients:
2 cups cornmeal
1 cup flour
1 cup yogurt
3 eggs
1/2 cup oil
1 baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
200 g cheese 

Instructions:
1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
2. Add eggs, yogurt, and oil.
3. Mix until smooth.
4. Pour the mixture into a baking pan.
5. Bake at 200°C (392°F) for 30–40 minutes.

Tip:
Let the cornbread cool for 10 minutes before serving.
